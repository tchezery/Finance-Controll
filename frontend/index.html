How to get the CSV link

  1. Open your Google Spreadsheet.
  2. Click on Share in the top right corner.
  3. Under "General access", change it to "Anyone with the link" (Viewer).
  4. Click on File > Share > Publish to web.
  5. In the "Link" tab, select your specific sheet (e.g. "Operations") or "Entire Document".
  6. In the second dropdown, select "Comma-separated values (.csv)".
  7. Click Publish, then copy the link provided.
  8. Paste the link into the configuration field below.
